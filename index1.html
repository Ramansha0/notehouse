Class 11 Chemistry Syllabus

Overview

Chemistry in Class 11 is divided into three main branches: Physical Chemistry, Organic Chemistry, and Inorganic Chemistry. It covers fundamental concepts that form the basis for advanced studies.

  • Some Basic Concepts of Chemistry – Mole concept, atomic and molecular masses.
  • Structure of Atom – Atomic models, quantum numbers, orbitals.
  • Classification of Elements and Periodicity in Properties – Modern periodic table, periodic trends.
  • Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure – Types of bonds, VSEPR theory, hybridization.
  • States of Matter – Gases, liquids, and intermolecular forces.
  • Thermodynamics – Laws of thermodynamics, enthalpy, entropy.
  • Equilibrium – Chemical and ionic equilibrium, Le Chatelier’s principle.
  • Redox Reactions – Oxidation, reduction, balancing redox reactions.
  • Hydrogen – Properties, uses, and compounds of hydrogen.
  • The s-Block Elements (Alkali and Alkaline Earth metals) – Properties, trends, and reactions.
  • Some p-Block Elements – Group 13 and 14 elements, properties, and compounds.
  • Organic Chemistry – Some Basic Principles and Techniques – Nomenclature, isomerism, purification techniques.
  • Hydrocarbons – Alkanes, alkenes, alkynes, aromatic hydrocarbons.
  • Environmental Chemistry – Pollution, greenhouse effect, acid rain.
